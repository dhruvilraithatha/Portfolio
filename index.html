# summary

What I actually do

I'm a DevOps and Site Reliability Engineer with 5+ years at Crest Data, working across cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, Kubernetes, and observability for banking, AML, and platform teams.

I started on the SRE side — administering Splunk Cloud, running enterprise data migrations, managing encryption keys and certificate lifecycles, and taking on-call. From there I moved into DevOps, where I now provision infrastructure with Terraform, automate delivery through Jenkins and GitHub Actions, and run workloads on Kubernetes with Helm.

Underneath all of it is the same instinct: make systems reproducible, observable, and boring in the best way — so deployments are predictable and incidents are short and rare.
